Theme : Biochemical Engineering and Food Engineering

Scientists try to understand nature. Engineers try to make things that do not exist in nature. Engineers stress invention. To embody an invention the engineer must put his idea in concrete terms, and design something that people can use. - Yuan-Cheng ("Bert") Fung All engineers try to help people by designing new inventions Biochemical engineers combine knowledge of biology, chemistry and engineering to create products from raw materials and develop the processes for achieving this. Food engineers design and develop systems for production, processing, distributing, and storing food and agricultural materials.
